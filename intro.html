Computers

Computer basically means processor. A computer is a very big source of information, entertainment and it is also very useful in sorts of stuff like this. A computer is very useful in schools homes and even offices especially. There are four main parts of the computer. CPU (Central Processing Unit), Monitor, Keyboard and Mouse. The CPU is the main processing part of the computer. It is also known as the brain of the computer having three processing chips called ALU (Arithmetic and Logic Unit), CU (Control Unit) and MU (Memory unit). Arithmetic and Logic unit does all the Mathematical calculations and controls different logics and “If” conditions that are given to different programs such as the physics in our games are also controlled by the ALU and the calculator is also an example of ALU. The CU controls and handles different programs or operating systems. If there is too much load on the CU, It might lag the computer or burn. MU contains and stores all the information that the user saves after working on the computer. If it is full, your computer might just warn you to clear it or plug in a USB Drive in the computer. The monitor is the main display of the computer that the computer uses to show the user what is happening on the computer. The screen shows pixels to make clear images. Pixels are tiny microscopic multicolored bulbs. Thousands of them come together to make a clear image. Keyboard has 74 keys with 26 letter keys, 10 number keys and 12 function keys, 2 shift keys, 2 Alt keys, 1 window key, 1 Enter key, 11 punctuation keys, 1 Backspace key, 1 capslock key, 1 Fn key, Two ctrl keys, 1 print screen key, 1 Esc key and 1 Delete key. The computer always reacts when we press any of the keys and sometimes it does’nt. A mouse is a tool of the computer we use to select objects from the screen. Wherever you move the mouse, A tiny pointer on the screen called the mouse pointer moves to the direction your mouse is moving. A computer is very useful in our lives nowadays and come up with different types like Desktop, Laptop, Smart phones and smart watches etc.
